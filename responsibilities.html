Game Captain Responsibilities

The Game Captain helps ensure weekly play runs smoothly by coordinating with the golf course, confirming the roster, and sharing results after play.

Before Game Day

  • Review the weekly signup sheet.
  • Confirm the number of tee times required.
  • Share the roster with the golf course.
  • Adjust tee time groupings if necessary.

On Game Day

  • Assist with player check-in if needed.
  • Communicate any last-minute cancellations to the course.
  • Answer questions about the weekly game format.
  • Ensure players understand the scoring format.

After Play

  • Collect scorecards if applicable.
  • Enter weekly results.
  • Share results with members.
  • Confirm winnings or payouts if applicable.
